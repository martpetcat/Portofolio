    Greetings and Welcome to my portfolio website.
    My name is Martin, I'm a Bulgarian student in Fontys ICT, Eindhoven. Currently a 1st year with a small technical yet expanding background. The title of my portfolio ...Always Work In Progress... is inspired from the personal challenges I had to overcome to get to the point where am somewhat satisfied with my website. I have found a way to express myself through this blend of creative and technical work. Being ambitious yet unexperienced has lead me to rebuild my portfolio countless times yet every time I learned something new - from wireframes to flexboxes (and so much more) This website took trial and error and it definitely got better along the way. I believe in individual identity and that the many small choices one makes along their path of creation and destruction leaves a mark to be discovered. The world is everchanging and so are we in the ways we output ourselves to the outside World. For all I've learned through my not so long time in the Netherlands, Eindhoven, Fontys ICT to be smart, efficient, stubborn in the way you output yourself is a constant work in progress...
